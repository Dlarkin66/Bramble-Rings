About Me


Hi, i'm Dylan! I'm from Boise, Idaho, and I am the owner and craftsman at Bramble Rings. I started Bramble Rings because I have a strong passion for woodworking and I wanted to share my jewelry with the world. I have been making unique one-of-a-kind rings for 6 years now, and plan to keep honing my craft and making customers happy!


All of my rings are handcrafted from start to finish with sustainably sourced materials. I carefully select each piece of wood, stone, and metal to ensure it meets my exceptional standards for quality. From there I carve the rings to shape and size on my lathe and polish them by hand, resulting in a truly unique ring that you won't find anywhere else.
